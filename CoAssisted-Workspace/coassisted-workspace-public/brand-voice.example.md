# Brand Voice Guidelines (example)

## Tone
- Direct, warm, no jargon
- Active voice
- 2nd person ("you") not 3rd

## Sign-offs
- "Thanks!" or "Cheers"

## Avoid
- Corporate buzzwords
- Em-dashes in casual chat
- Excessive emoji

(Replace with your own voice guidelines. Used by the brand-voiced
outbound message composer in the receipt + invoice extractors.)
